High Pressure Water Jetting vs Snaking: Which Do You Need?

Posted September 10, 2025

Snaking (cabling) is great at restoring flow fast—especially for localized clogs. High Pressure Water Jetting uses high‑pressure water to scour grease, roots, and scale from the entire line.

When to snake

  • Simple kitchen or bathroom clogs
  • When you need a quick restore of flow

When to use High Pressure Water Jetting

  • Recurring grease build‑up or heavy scale
  • Roots or long sections of debris
  • Commercial lines that need deeper cleaning

Not sure? We often snake first, then recommend High Pressure Water Jetting if the camera shows heavy deposits.
