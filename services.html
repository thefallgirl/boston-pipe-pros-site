Rats & Sewer Intrusions

Rats can enter through damaged or uncapped sewer laterals and floor drains. We diagnose entry points and secure your system so pests can’t use your drains as a highway.

How we help

  • Camera inspections to locate broken or open connections
  • Sealing and proper trap/cleanout caps
  • Under‑slab spot repairs and pipe lining to close gaps
  • High Pressure Water Jetting to clear debris that attracts pests

Prevention tips

  • Keep floor drain traps primed (water in the trap blocks odors and pests)
  • Replace missing cleanout caps and broken fixtures promptly
  • Avoid flushing food waste and grease—both attract rodents
  • Schedule periodic camera inspections in older homes
